0004 Classroom check

Trolley Setup:

1.  Load glassware:
    *   12 × 100 mL beakers
    *   12 × 250 mL beakers
    *   12 × 600 mL beakers
    *   5 × 10 mL measuring cylinders
    *   5 × 100 mL measuring cylinders
2.  Load consumables:
    *   1 bundle of paper towels
    *   3 boxes of gloves
3.  Load utensils:
    *   Spatulas
    *   Test tube holders (wood and metal)
    *   Watch glasses
    *   Tongs
    *   Stirring rods
    *   Funnels
4.  Add other items:
    *   Clipboard and paper for notes
    *   At least 5 newspapers

Restocking Steps:

1.  Put on gloves.
2.  Check lab coats: There should be 5 per room.
3.  Check front desk:
    *   6 boxes of matches
    *   5 goggles
    *   At least 3 boxes of gloves
    *   1 bundle of paper towels
4.  Move to lab utensils area:
    *   Ensure at least 12 of each: spatulas, test tube holders (wood and metal), watch glasses, tongs, stirring rods, funnels.
    *   Ensure at least 5 newspapers.
5.  Move to side bench:
    *   Check glassware: At least 12 of each type listed above.
    *   Inspect for broken, dirty, or stained items; retain for later.
6.  Check extras drawer under lab benches (covered later).
7.  Sweep rubbish by hand from benches.
8.  Check each bench for:
    *   Tripod
    *   Bunsen burner
    *   Gauze mat
    *   Pipe clay triangle
    *   Silicone bench mat
9.  If broken or dirty, retain and make a note.
10. Place any extras found on top of the bench.
11. After all benches are checked:
    *   Collect extras and redistribute to benches missing items.
12. Make notes of missing items, new orders, and other observations on the clipboard.
